Ashraf Aman is a Pakistani mountaineer, adventurer, and engineer. In 1977, he became the first Pakistani to reach the summit of K2.

Essential Tips:

Check The Weather

It will give you the chance to change plans
instead of getting surprised on the trail.

Wear the right shoes and socks:

Painful feet can ruin a hike.
Invest in quality hiking shoes and socks.

Pace yourself:

You may feel like powering forward like a hero.However,
you’ll be a zero by the end of the day if you don’t pace yourself.

Dress for success:

Once your feet are taken care of,
dressing right is key to comfort on your hike.

Leave no trace:

Take time to read the Leave No Trace Seven Principals
and follow them. It’s up to every outdoor enthusiast to take care of our natural spaces.
